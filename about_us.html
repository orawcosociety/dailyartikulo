About the ORAWCO Society

The Oratorical Writing and Communication (ORAWCO) Society club of San Vicente - San Francisco High School.

Established in 2023, the Oratorical Writing and Communication (ORAWCO) Society club of San Vicente - San Francisco High School ensures enthusiastic, justifiable, and reliable sources for people who sought answers--justice, truth, equality, and knowledge.

A nonprofit organization that only meant for demonstrating and providing articles towards people, especially for the learners of San Vicente - San Francisco High School. With the power of writing, oral, and communication--we able to hold an activity called "Daily Artikulo" which meant a big respect, accountancy, and service towards learners and to the community.

All writers in our activity powered by the ORAWCO Society of SVSF HS are cooperating willingly; therefore, it is their decision to acknowledge and to participate with us.

With the help of the San Vicente - San Francisco High School, we managed to run and hold an activity that shall embody our spirit as a writer, found a way to speak up, and to determine the youth to stand up and use the power of writing and speech for truth, justice, freedom, and equality.

We, the Oratorical Writing and Communication (ORAWCO) Society club do hereby accept the consequences and responsibilities regarding our article making. Hence the name "Daily Artikulo," a bestowed gratitude from the society towards the school of San Vicente - San Francisco High School.
