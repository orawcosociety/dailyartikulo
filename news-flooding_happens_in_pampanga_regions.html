Flooding happens in Pampanga regions

Published on July 22, 2025 | By Jersey Lois M. Dela Rosa

Amidst the typhoon, the Pampanga regions are facing an orange warning which causes flooding, according to the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC), on July 21, 2025.

The municipality of Macabebe, Pampanga currently facing flooding in certain barangays.

The barangay of Saplad David, Macabebe, Pampanga faced a severe flooding, the residents are seeking help for a place to stay, and food-kits as their house are badly affected.

The municipality of Milanin, Pampanga also faces a problem in flooding, some schools, houses, and roads were submerged into the flood.

According to the Local Government Units (LGUs), those residents of Pampanga who were affected in flooding on Pampanga can reach them to request help for supplies, and evacuation to stay.
